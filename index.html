Frequently Asked Questions

Our programs are open to high school graduates or equivalent. No prior legal experience is required. We welcome career changers and those looking to advance in the legal field.

Program lengths vary from 8 to 18 weeks depending on the specialization. We offer flexible scheduling including part-time, evening, weekend, and online options to accommodate working professionals.

We maintain a 94% job placement rate within 6 months of graduation. Our career services team provides ongoing support including resume review, interview preparation, and direct connections to hiring partners.
